# ECONOMICS.md

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

A startup spending $500+/month on AI tools is the target customer. If Credex can offer 20% savings on $500/month = $100/month saved for the customer. Credex margin on discounted credits is estimated at 15-20%. On a $500/month customer that's $75-100/month in gross profit. Annual value per customer = ~$900-1200.

Conservative estimate: **$1,000 ARR per converted customer.**

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage                                | Rate | Numbers                    |
| ------------------------------------ | ---- | -------------------------- |
| Visitor → audit completed            | 30%  | 1000 visitors → 300 audits |
| Audit completed → email captured     | 20%  | 300 audits → 60 leads      |
| Email captured → consultation booked | 15%  | 60 leads → 9 bookings      |
| Consultation → credit purchase       | 40%  | 9 bookings → 3-4 customers |

From 1000 visitors → 3-4 paying Credex customers.

## What Makes This Profitable

- CAC is effectively $0 in organic channels
- Each customer worth ~$1,000 ARR
- Even 10 customers/month = $120,000 ARR
- Breakeven at first customer given $0 CAC

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**What would have to be true:**

- 1,000 paying Credex customers at $1,000 ARR each
- From 300 audits/day → ~60 leads/day → ~9 bookings/day → ~3-4 customers/day
- That requires ~10,000 visitors/day to the tool
- Achievable through: HN virality, SEO on "AI tool cost" queries, Credex email list, referral sharing of audit results

**The math:**

- Month 1-3: 0-50 customers via organic ($0-50k ARR)
- Month 4-9: 50-300 customers via SEO + referrals ($50-300k ARR)
- Month 10-18: 300-1000 customers via paid + partnerships ($300k-1M ARR)

**Rough inputs — all estimates. Real numbers depend on Credex's actual margin and customer LTV.**
